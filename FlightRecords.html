Flight records

The YF-118 g first flew on September 11, 1996,
at Groom Lake. Its initial set of flights face some issues.
It had created a lot of drag and faced stability issues
due to its design. The yf-23 on the other hand was
unveiled to the public to compete the yf-23. There are
two prototypes, the original being the Black Widow II
and the other being the Grey Ghost.
